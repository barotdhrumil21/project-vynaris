# Draft a document

## Structure first
Before writing prose, produce an outline — 4–7 bullets. Confirm it's the right shape by saving it to `private/draft-<slug>.md`.

## Draft
Write to `public/draft-<slug>.md` (unless marked confidential). Use:
- **Headline / TL;DR** up top — 1–2 sentences
- **Context** — 1 paragraph, not more
- **The core** — 3–5 sections with clear subheads
- **Asks / next steps** — explicit, bulleted

## Match the voice of the person
Read their `memory.md` if you have prior drafts there. Match length, formality, and their preferred sentence rhythm.

## Anti-patterns
- No throat-clearing intros ("I hope this finds you well").
- No hedging filler ("it might be worth considering…").
- No corporate euphemisms. Say "we lost the deal" not "deal outcomes were suboptimal".

## Wrap up
Post to feed: "Drafted <doc>. Link: <path>. Happy to revise."
